About FakeGuard

FakeGuard is an AI‑powered system designed to help users identify fake and suspicious social media accounts. As online platforms grow, fake profiles are increasingly used for scams, misinformation, and identity misuse.

The system analyzes account‑related information using machine learning techniques to classify profiles as real or fake. By presenting results in a clear and user‑friendly manner, FakeGuard helps users make informed decisions and stay safe online.
